Word Meadow Privacy Policy

Grobe Games LLC • Effective Date: April 3, 2026

Summary: Word Meadow does not collect personal information directly. The app uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements, displays ads through a third-party ad network, and uses Apple's crash reporting to help us fix bugs. We do not sell or share your personal data.

1. Who We Are

This privacy policy covers the mobile application Word Meadow ("the App"), developed and published by Grobe Games LLC, a limited liability company organized in the United States. In this policy, "we," "us," and "our" refer to Grobe Games LLC.

2. Information We Collect

Word Meadow itself does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the App uses the following third-party services that may collect certain data:

3. Apple Game Center

Word Meadow integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. When you use Game Center features:

  • Your Game Center display name and scores may be visible to other players on leaderboards
  • Your achievements are synced through your Apple ID
  • Game Center data is managed by Apple in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy

You can disable Game Center in your device's Settings at any time.

4. Advertising

Word Meadow displays ads provided by a third-party ad network. The ad provider may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as the Identifier for Advertisers, or IDFA, if you have granted permission)
  • General device information (device model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, taps)

You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. The App respects Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework and will request your permission before tracking you across other apps and websites.

5. Crash Reporting

Word Meadow uses crash reporting to help us identify and fix bugs. When a crash occurs, the following data may be collected:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information

This data does not include personal information and is used solely to improve app stability. You can opt out of sharing crash data through your device's Settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

6. Data We Do Not Collect

To be clear, Word Meadow does not:

  • Collect your name, email, phone number, or location
  • Require account creation or login (beyond optional Game Center)
  • Store gameplay data on external servers
  • Sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

7. Children's Privacy

Word Meadow is designed to be a family-friendly game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The third-party services used in the App (Game Center, advertising, crash reporting) are managed by their respective providers and are subject to their own privacy policies and age-related restrictions.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at privacy@grobegames.com and we will take steps to address the issue.

8. Data Retention

Since we do not directly collect personal data, we do not retain personal information. Game progress is stored locally on your device. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since Word Meadow does not directly collect personal data, such requests should be directed to the relevant third-party service providers (Apple, ad network). If you have questions, contact us and we'll help point you in the right direction.

10. App Store Privacy Details (Nutrition Label)

In accordance with Apple's App Store requirements, the following summarizes the data practices of Word Meadow as disclosed in our App Store privacy "nutrition label":

Data Used to Track You

The following data, collected by our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob), may be used to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies:

  • Identifiers — Device ID, Advertising ID
  • Usage Data — Product interaction (ad views, clicks, dismissals)
  • Diagnostics — Performance data, crash logs

Data Linked to You

The following data, collected by our third-party advertising partner, may be linked to your identity:

  • Identifiers — Device ID, Advertising ID
  • Usage Data — Product interaction
  • Diagnostics — Performance data, crash logs

Purposes

All data listed above is collected and used by our third-party advertising partner for the following purposes:

  • Third-Party Advertising — Serving, personalizing, and measuring advertisements
Important: Grobe Games LLC does not directly collect any of the data listed above. All data collection is performed by the Google AdMob SDK integrated into the App for advertising purposes. You can limit tracking by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt or by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in your device settings. You can eliminate forced ads entirely by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase (rewarded ads, which are always optional, remain available).

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the App or applicable laws. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
