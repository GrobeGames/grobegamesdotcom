Photon Light Quest

Bend light. Solve puzzles. Discover the magic of color.

Step into a dazzling world where light is your superpower! Tap, drag, and rotate mirrors, prisms, and magical elements to guide beams of light through mind-bending puzzles. Watch white light split into brilliant rainbows, bounce off mirrors at just the right angle, and unlock pathways you never saw coming.

Every level is a colorful brain-teaser that'll make you think, experiment, and celebrate when that beam finally hits home. Whether you're 8 or 80, Photon Light Quest will have you seeing the world in a whole new light.

  • Intuitive tap-and-drag controls anyone can pick up
  • Progressively challenging puzzles that keep your brain buzzing
  • Stunning light effects and vibrant color mechanics
  • Family-friendly fun for all ages
